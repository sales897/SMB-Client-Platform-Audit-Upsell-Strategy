🔗 Connect Google Sheets

Publish your sheet to the web as CSV, then paste that link below. The hub will fetch live data and rebuild every chart, KPI, and table automatically.

📌 How to get the link:
1. In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web
2. Select the SMBs sheet/tab specifically (not "Entire Document")
3. Choose format: Comma-separated values (.csv)
4. Click Publish → copy the generated link
5. Paste it above and click Connect
📋 Required columns: Business Name, Yelp, Google LSA, Voice Agent, Thumbtack, Chat Widget, Contact Form, Acculynx, Builder Prime, Go High Level, Housecall Pro  (use YES/NO or TRUE/FALSE)
